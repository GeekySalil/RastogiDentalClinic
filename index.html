Quick Clarifications

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Root Canal Treatment (RCT) painful?
Modern Root Canal Treatments (RCT) at RASTOGI DENTAL CLINIC are virtually painless. Using highly premium rotatory systems and optimized local anesthesia, our patients experience absolute comfort resembling standard cavity fillings.
Do I need a pre-booked appointment?
While we accommodate walk-ins, we highly suggest securing an appointment slot to minimize transit times and ensure our experts have sufficient dedicated time to handle your clinical requirements.
Are dental implants completely safe?
Yes. At RASTOGI DENTAL CLINIC, we use bio-compatible premium grade titanium alloys. Implants are highly predictable, long-lasting, and structurally secure.
What are the consultation timings?
Our consultation grids run Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM and 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM. On Sundays, we are open for Morning OPD from 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM.
